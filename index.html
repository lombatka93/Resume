Summary

Consultant Engineer with over 8 years of industry experience.

Having worked with various clients over a wide range of projects has enabled me to develop strong abilities in various aspects of civil and structural engineering including analysis and design, survey work, report writing, preparing CAD and sketch drawings, and attending project meetings from concept to completion.

I stay on top of the industry by continually improving my skills and knowledge through work-study and dedicated professional development. This process has been vital in enabling me to efficiently manage challenges within any project I face while staying up to date with the evolving construction industry.

My work has helped me gain experience with both new builds and the refurbishment of existing structures, often comprising a conservation element with complex challenges. I enjoy being an integral part of a well-established team, collaborative working approach on various challenges with multidisciplinary project teams has allowed me to develop and expand both professional and engineering skills.

I hold professionalism and integrity to a high degree within my work, something that I regard as a key aspect of holding a membership with the Institution of Structural Engineers.
